PHY102: Complete Cheat Sheet

electric charge · DC circuits · electromagnetic induction

Week 1: Introduction

  • Unit Conversions and Constants 1 uC = 10^-6 C, 1 mA = 10^-3 A, k = 1/(4 pi e0) = 8.99 x 10^9 N m^2 C^-2.
  • General Strategy Start from known variables, write governing law, substitute SI units, then solve.

Week 2: Forces in Nature and Electrostatics

  • Coulomb's Law F = k |q1 q2| / r^2.
  • Vector Form F12 = k q1 q2 r_hat / r^2.
  • Superposition Principle F_net = F1 + F2 + ... + Fn (vector sum).

Week 3: Electric Field and Potential

  • Electric Field E = F/q, for point charge E = kq/r^2.
  • Electric Potential V = W/q, for point charge V = kq/r.
  • Potential Difference and Field E = Delta V / d (uniform field).
  • Gauss's Law Psi_E = integral E . dA = Q_enclosed / e0.

Week 4: Capacitance and Electric Dipoles

  • Capacitance C = Q/V, parallel plate C = e0 A/d.
  • Energy Stored in Capacitor U = 1/2 C V^2 = 1/2 QV = Q^2/(2C).
  • Electric Dipole Moment p = qd.
  • Torque on Dipole tau = pE sin(theta).
  • Electric Field Energy Density u = 1/2 e0 E^2.

Week 5: DC Circuits

  • Current I = Delta Q / Delta t.
  • Ohm's Law V = IR.
  • Resistance of Conductor R = rho L/A.
  • Resistors in Series R_eq = R1 + R2 + ....
  • Resistors in Parallel 1/R_eq = 1/R1 + 1/R2 + ....
  • Electric Power P = IV = I^2R = V^2/R.

Week 6: Magnetic Fields

  • Magnetic Force on Charge F = qvB sin(theta).
  • Lorentz Force F = q(E + v x B).
  • Force on Current-Carrying Wire F = BIL sin(theta).
  • Biot-Savart Law dB = (mu0/4pi) (I dl x r_hat)/r^2.
  • Ampere's Law integral B . dl = mu0 I_enclosed.
  • Magnetic Energy Density u_B = B^2/(2mu0).

Week 7: EMF and Electromagnetic Induction

  • Faraday's Law emf = -N Delta Phi_B / Delta t.
  • Magnetic Flux Phi_B = BA cos(theta).
  • Self-Inductance emf_L = -L dI/dt.
  • Energy in Inductor U = 1/2 L I^2.
  • Transformer Ratio Vs/Vp = Ns/Np and Ip/Is = Ns/Np (ideal).

Week 8: Maxwell's Equations

  • Gauss for Electricity integral E . dA = Q_enclosed/e0.
  • Gauss for Magnetism integral B . dA = 0.
  • Faraday-Maxwell Law integral E . dl = - dPhi_B/dt.
  • Ampere-Maxwell Law integral B . dl = mu0 I_enclosed + mu0 e0 dPhi_E/dt.

Week 9: Electromagnetic Oscillations and Waves

  • Wave Speed c = 1/sqrt(mu0 e0).
  • Wave Relation c = f lambda.
  • Field Ratio in EM Wave E0 = cB0.
  • LC Oscillator Frequency omega = 1/sqrt(LC), f = 1/(2pi sqrt(LC)).

Week 10: AC Voltages and Currents

  • RMS Values V_rms = V0/sqrt(2), I_rms = I0/sqrt(2).
  • Reactances X_L = omega L, X_C = 1/(omega C).
  • Impedance (Series RLC) Z = sqrt(R^2 + (X_L - X_C)^2).
  • AC Ohm's Law I_rms = V_rms/Z.
  • Average Power P_avg = V_rms I_rms cos(phi), cos(phi) = R/Z.
